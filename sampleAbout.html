About the Portal

The Campus Complaint Tracker Portal is designed to simplify the process of reporting and resolving infrastructure and facility-related issues within the university campus. It enables students to raise complaints related to classrooms, hostels, labs, or other facilities efficiently, while allowing administrators to track, prioritize, and address them transparently.

Our mission is to foster a more responsive and transparent environment where student voices are heard and campus services continually improve. With easy access, real-time tracking, feedback options, and analytics for recurring issues — we aim to make the campus experience smoother for everyone.
